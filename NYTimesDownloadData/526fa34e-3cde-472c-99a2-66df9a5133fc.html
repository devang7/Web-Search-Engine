From its first run in September of 1851 to the present day, The New York Times has had its finger on the pulse of the world, and has offered up-to-the-minute updates on the day’s most pressing issues and stories.

This 100% Botticino marble Times clock not only adds character to your home, but by displaying a snapshot of history, it offers a glance at the myriad of issues that seized the world’s attention on that particular day.

The marble’s natural veining, small fissures, coloring and tumbling all add to the one-of-a-kind beauty of this natural stone clock.

This charming New York Times clock measures 8" x 8" and makes a unique and personal gift. Get the TOP HALF of any New York Times front page on your marble clock.

Because of the square dimensions, the bottom portion of the page won't appear on the clock.

Keep in mind that important events usually hit the paper the day AFTER the date of the event.

Clock comes ready to hang and in a gift box. AA battery required (not included).

Also available: A miniature easel made of heavyweight forged-steel to present your clock on a desk or table.

Please allow 5 business days for production. Express shipping will not expedite production time.


Because this personalized item is custom-made for you, all sales are final and this product cannot be returned, refunded or exchanged.
