Since its inception in 1851, The New York Times has worked every day to assemble a detailed picture of our world. Now you can piece together the headlines with this New York Times jigsaw puzzle, made from any historic front page of your choosing.

With these unique jigsaw puzzles, you can dive into the harrowing tale of the sinking of the R.M.S. Titanic, get wrapped up in the excitement of V-E Day or the end of the Civil War, learn all about Lucky Lindbergh's daring flight across the Atlantic, or see the stories that made The New York Times front page on your birthday or anniversary unfold before your eyes.

The jigsaw puzzle of the day will challenge master puzzle enthusiasts and inspire budding puzzlers alike. Wow your loved one with a special gift: Give a jigsaw puzzle featuring historic headlines from his or her birthday, anniversary or other special day.

Jigsaw Puzzle Details


Each puzzle, available with 300, 500 or 1,000 pieces, is made using advanced puzzle-making equipment and sturdy 1.6mm-thick chipboard. The 500-piece puzzle is our most popular version; the 1,000-piece puzzle has similar-size pieces and is twice as large; and the 300-piece puzzle has big, easy-to-hold pieces, perfect for little ones and experienced puzzle makers to enjoy together. Made in the USA. Pages after 1998 are in full color.

Ordering Details


Each puzzle is custom-printed on a per order basis. Ships within three business days.

Because this personalized item is custom-made for you, all sales are final and this product cannot be returned, refunded or exchanged.
