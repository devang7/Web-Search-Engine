This personalized New York Times book contains every anniversary front page from the day you said "I do" to this year's anniversary.

A touching keepsake memory book for parents, grandparents or for you and your spouse to enjoy together, this hardbound book features your recipients' names in gold lettering on the cover.

See how the earth-shaking historic events that dominated the news of the day overlap with your anniversaries each year you shared together with this commemorative homage to a romance for the ages.

The book also contains a fascinating 22-page timeline detailing more than 1,300 news and cultural events from 1900â€“2009, 10 blank pages invite you to add your own personal mementos, along with a magnifier, a certificate of authenticity and a special New York Times coin.

The perfect companion showpiece, the anniversary book gift coin is made of solid bronze and is engraved with the book's selected date on one side and iconic New York Times Tower on the other. Made in the USA.

Book measures 12" x 15". Coin measures 1.5".
