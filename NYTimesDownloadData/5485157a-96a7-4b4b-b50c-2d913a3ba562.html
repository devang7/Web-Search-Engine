Though the oak barrel has been associated with wine making for millennia, its use in aging wine owes more to serendipity than science. Roman troops, as they expanded the Empire, always traveled with wine, which they transported in clay amphorae. Cumbersome and fragile, the Romans were quick to dispense with the amphorae when they discovered the Gauls using oak wood barrels to transport beer. Oak proved abundant in the forests of Europe, was water tight, yet easy to bend into a barrel shape. Over the course of transporting their wines, it was discovered that the oak imparted new pleasant qualities to the wine, softening and smoothing it, while also improving the taste. Thus began the practice of aging wine in oak.

Continue this noble tradition with your very own wine cask, handmade from American white oak and personalized with the name, date and city of your choosing. “Vineyards” and “Est.” appear automatically.

Like any oak barrel, it can age red or white wine or hold other beverages like beer, tequila or rum.

The barrels, available in 2 or 5-liter sizes, are built using oak staves, just like full-size barrels, and are reinforced with steel hoops. Each barrel comes with a wooden stand.

2-liter barrel: 7.25" long x 5.5" diameter.
5-liter barrel: 9.75" long x 8.75" diameter.
10-liter barrel: 11.75" long x 10.5" diameter.

Because of natural variations of wood, each barrel is unique and may differ from the images shown here.

Each is laser personalized. Vineyards and Est. come automatically. Made in the U.S.A.

Like full-size barrels, this mini-barrel needs to be cured when using for the first time. This is normal. Simply follow the instructions to seal the barrel.

Usually ships in 2 - 3 business days.
