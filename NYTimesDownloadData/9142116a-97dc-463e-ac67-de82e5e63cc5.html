The New York Times has created a beautiful, sepia-toned pushpin world map that is sure to delight any explorer. Simply mark the destinations of your life with a pin, and watch as the world is dotted by your memories: cross-country road trips, back-packing through Europe or the thrill of that African safari. This personalized travel map with pins is a way to keep track of your global adventures, mark the locations of friends and family, or pin the destinations you hope to someday reachand it enhances your home as a handsome wall display at the same time.

Pushpin Travel Map Details


This foam-core map is professionally finished in a high-end wooden frame with handsome beading. It includes 100 color pushpins, so you can mark your favorite past trips, exotic locales you've yet to explore, and pinpoint locations of friends and family around the world. Recently enhanced, the map includes photos from The New York Times of famous destinations along the bottom, including the Great Wall of China, Eiffel Tower and Mount Rushmore.

The pushpin world map has wonderful nostalgic accents, including travel icons seemingly circling the world at the bottom, which says World Travels and allows for additional words of personalization. Specify up to 20 personalization characters of your choice, such as your first or last name

Comes ready to hang. Map dimensions are approximately 24" x 36"; frame dimensions are approximately 26" x 38".

Ordering Details


Ships within two business days from Vermont. If you are ordering from Hawaii or Alaska, you will be contacted for additional shipping charges.

Because this personalized item is custom-made for you, all sales are final and this product cannot be returned, refunded or exchanged.
