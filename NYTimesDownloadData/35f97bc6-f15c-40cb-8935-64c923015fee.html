Through reprinted New York Times pages, this personalized football team history book captures a teams glory days, biggest games and greatest players. It contains profiles, Hall of Fame announcements, obituaries and rarely seen pages pulled from the renowned New York Times archives.

Now updated with 2017 Super Bowl coverage, this book retraces the history of the National Football League team you select, making it a personalized football book for your team. And its a great gift for football fans: the football lover in your life will get a kick out of seeing his or her name embossed on the cover of this, the best of football history books.

Football Book Details


All professional football teams are available as well as a history of the Super Bowl. Measuring 12" x 15", each hardbound football book comes personalized with your recipient's name stamped in gold foil on the handsome leatherette cover.

Books are 70-80 pages and the covers (blue, black, red or green) reflect the team's basic color. Pages are mainly black and white and printed on off-white paper, but select color pages are included after 1998.

Comes with magnifier and certificate of authenticity. Made in U.S.A.

Ordering Details


Because this personalized football gift is custom-made for you, all sales are final and cannot be returned, refunded or exchanged.
