Celebrate this remarkable year in baseball—including a historic World Series win for the Chicago Cubs—with the Baseball History Book. This customizable book retraces the Major League baseball history of the team you select, including MVP profiles, Hall of Fame announcements, obituaries and rarely seen pages pulled from the extensive New York Times archives. A timeless tribute to your home team, this beautiful, handmade leatherette history of baseball book makes a great gift for baseball lovers who have everything.

Baseball History Book Details


All 30 Major League baseball teams are available, plus two teams of yesteryear: the Brooklyn Dodgers and New York Giants. Baseball books range from 70 to 80 pages, and the covers (blue, black, red or green) reflect the team's basic color.

Measuring 12" x 15", each hardbound history of baseball book comes personalized with your recipient's name stamped in gold foil on the handsome leatherette cover. Made in the USA. Pages are mainly black and white and printed on off-white paper, but select color pages are included after 1998.

Comes with magnifier and certificate of authenticity. Made in the U.S.A.

Ordering Details


Because this personalized baseball gift is custom-made for you, all sales are final and this product cannot be returned, refunded or exchanged.
