  • 1. How do I access my Times Insider benefits?
    Back to top

    You can access Times Insider content by navigating to the Times Insider homepage. Times Insider content, in addition to TBooks and Complimentary Subscription benefits, can also be accessed in My Account.

    Learn more about becoming a Times Insider subscriber in our Digital Subscriptions FAQ.

  • 2. How do I download my free TBooks?
    Back to top

    Times Insider subscribers have access to our full library of more than 120 e-book titles, free in the NYTStore. Two new TBooks are made available on the first of every month.

    To download your free TBooks, you can also visit My Account and click on the TBooks module on the bottom of the page or on the right side of the Times Insider homepage.

    For more help with TBooks and how to download them, visit the support page.

  • 3. How do I listen to Times Insider podcasts?
    Back to top

    Times Insider podcasts can be found on the Times Insider homepage. They can also be accessed via the Insider Podcasts page or in My Account. You can also subscribe to Times Insider podcasts from the Podcast link on the Times Insider homepage.

  • 4. How can I attend Times Insider events?
    Back to top

    As a Times Insider subscriber, you are signed up for our event invitations which you will receive via email based on your region. If you are an Insider subscriber and not receiving event invitations, please contact our support team at timesinsidercare@nytimes.com. To learn more about upcoming and past Insider events, visit the Times Insider Events page.

  • 5. How will I be notified of new Insider content every week?
    Back to top

    Every week, Times Insider subscribers will receive the Weekly Wrap email newsletter, which provides a way to catch up on all of the new Insider content you may have missed. You can unsubscribe from this newsletter at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of the newsletter. The latest Times Insider content will also be available on the Times Insider section as well as on your mobile devices. If you are a Times Insider subscriber and not receiving the Weekly Wrap newsletter, please contact us at timesinsidercare@nytimes.com.

  • 6. How do I use my Complimentary Subscription benefits?
    Back to top

    Times Insider subscribers are able to share the All Digital Access portion of their subscription with up to two other people. To provide Complimentary Subscriptions to two other people, visit the Subscriptions section of My Account. Under Complimentary Subscription, enter the email address of the intended recipient and click Go.

    Complimentary Subscriptions can be revoked and shared again with someone else in the Subscriptions section of My Account.

  • 7. How can I access Times Insider on my mobile device?
    Back to top

    As a Times Insider subscriber, you can access Times Insider on both Android and iOS devices. If you have the Times app on your iOS or Android and are logged in to your account, Times Insider will appear as a new section. If you do not have the app, you can also access Times Insider from your mobile device by going to the Times Insider homepage on mobile.

  • 8. How can I cancel my Times Insider subscription?
    Back to top

    You must call Customer Care at (800) 698-4637 to cancel your Times Insider subscription.
