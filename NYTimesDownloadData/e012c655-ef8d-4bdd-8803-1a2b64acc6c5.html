The Death of Freddie Gray and Its Aftermath

Freddie Gray, a 25-year-old in Baltimore, died of a spinal cord injury while in police custody. How Gray received the injury and a delay in receiving medical attention, added to the mystery surrounding his death, setting off several days of violent protests and revived the controversy of police brutality toward black men.
