Every year, the Ultimate Birthday Book is far and away our top-selling gift. It was inspired by a request from a prominent New Yorker to present a personalized compendium of The New York Times front pages to his mother for her 100th birthday.

The Ultimate Birthday Book provides a magical journey through history with the most important world-gripping headlines from a special date, starting with the year the recipient was born, through today. If your special date is August 3, 1925, the Ultimate Birthday Book will include the front page from that date, and the front pages from August 3 every year following. It showcases the most important articles and photos from America's preeminent newspaper. This special book also includes a detailed timeline of the important historical events of the last century.

Exclusively available from The New York Times Store, our Birthday Book is customized with a name and date in gold lettering on the sleek, hardbound cover for a personal touch, plus commemorative accessories to treasure. Make this collectible your loved ones favorite gift this year.

Ultimate Birthday Book Details


The Birthday Book is proudly made in Vermont and measures 13 x 15. It displays "The Times of Your Life" on the cover. The package also includes the following:

  • A beautiful gift box (14" x 16" x 2.5") with a New York City skyline design.
  • A fascinating 22-page timeline detailing more than 1,300 news and cultural events from 1900-2009.
  • An engraved commemorative solid bronze coin (1.5), engraved with your selected date, with The New York Times headlines on one side and the renowned New York Times Tower on the reverse.
  • Four additional front pages from the year you were born, showing that year's biggest events.
  • 10 blank pages for personal mementos.
  • A handy, page-size magnifier (8.5" x 11").
  • A certificate of authenticity.
