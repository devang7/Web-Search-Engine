This personalized money clip wallet is made in the USA of 22 gauge brass composed of 85% copper and 15% zinc. The brass comes from a Chicago mill where it is melted down and turned into a brass coil about 1" wide. The coil is then stamped into personalized money clips with a 16 ton hydraulic press, and then each is pierced to create the distinctive finger on the inside and bent in half to create the clip.

Artisans at Owen & Fred — a New York-based men's design brand founded in 2012 — then buff the brass money clip with ultra-fine grit sandpaper to bevel the edges and give the metal a soft, brushed finish.

The monogrammed money clip displays initials that are laser etched into the brass permanently and will not fade.

Monogram money clip measures 2 x 1. Gift boxed.

Because this personalized item is custom-made for you, all sales are final and your money clip cannot be returned, refunded or exchanged.
