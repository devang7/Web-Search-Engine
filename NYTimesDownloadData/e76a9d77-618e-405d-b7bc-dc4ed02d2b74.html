War Poster Collection

Created to rally support for the war effort, these war posters capture the public sentiment at the time and are important pieces of history. Featuring World War 1 propaganda posters and World War 2 propaganda posters, this war poster collection contains potent slogans and imagery that encouraged every American to do their part to win the wars on the battlefield and at home.
