This black matte coffee cup commemorates the founding of The New York Times and celebrates its storied history. Coffee has long been associated with stimulating intellectual conversations; since the Enlightenment, coffeehouses have been places for people to meet, socialize, and discuss the important issues of the day. Continue this tradition at home with your own coffee cup and the Times in hand or on your tablet. With its unique barrel shape, this ironstone ceramic mug can hold 16-ounces of your favorite hot beverage.

Black matte ironstone ceramic mug with a large C-handle and unique barrel shape. 16-ounce capacity. 4" x 4 1/2".

Wash by hand only; not microwave or dishwasher safe.
