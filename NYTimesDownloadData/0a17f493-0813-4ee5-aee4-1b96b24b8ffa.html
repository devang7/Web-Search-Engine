Build your own community in an afternoon with this spectacular bird’s-eye view that makes you feel as if you're panning over the one unforgettable place that pulls your heartstrings.

Custom made to the address that you provide, these hometown jigsaw puzzles use the latest aerial imagery to reveal local landmarks, houses, swimming pools, trees and cars in full color and stunning detail.

Select your old stomping grounds or a landmark in your favorite city — from Times Square in the Big Apple to Hollywood Boulevard in Los Angeles — and see your favorite rural, suburban or urban spot spring to life like you've never seen it before. Made in the USA.

Our 500-piece aerial puzzle covers an area approximately 0.4 miles wide by 0.6 miles long. The 1,000 piece covers a slightly larger area - approximately .75 miles by 1.2 miles.

Each custom aerial map jigsaw puzzle is printed on a per order basis.

500 piece puzzles ship within 3 business days; 1,000 piece puzzles ship in 5 – 7 business days.

Because this personalized item is custom-made for you, all sales are final and this product cannot be returned, refunded or exchanged.
