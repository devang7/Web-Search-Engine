No two stones are alike, making this a one-of-a-kind keepsake with far more character than found on plain ceramic tiles. The marbles natural veining, small fissures, coloring and tumbling all add to the beauty of this natural stone.

Provide any U.S. address and well send a clock, showing a map of your area. The topographical map will show three square miles, including your selected location, main streets and landmarks. Your address is marked with a compass rose.

Handcrafted in New England from marble from the Mediterranean region. Clock features Times logo and lists the street and town you selected.

Includes attachment on back for hanging on a wall. Comes in gift box. This product is only available for U.S. addresses and zip codes only.

AA battery operated; battery not included. Please allow 5 business days for production.

ALSO AVAILABLE: For an additional $18.95 this product comes with a metal easel, made of heavyweight forged-steel, to present this unique and beautiful clock on a desk or table.


Because this personalized item is custom-made for you, all sales are final and cannot be returned, refunded or exchanged.
