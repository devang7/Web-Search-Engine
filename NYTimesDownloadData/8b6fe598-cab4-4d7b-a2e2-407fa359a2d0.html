Kindle

You can read the award-winning journalism of The New York Times on your Kindle devices. There are apps for both Kindle e-readers and the Kindle Fire.

  • 1. Is there a New York Times app for the Kindle Fire or Kindle e-reader?
    Back to top

    Yes. You can read NYTimes content on both your Kindle Fire and Kindle e-reader.

    The New York Times for Kindle e-reader features articles from the newspaper, sent directly to your device each day. The New York Times for Kindle Fire is an app that you can download from the Kindle Fire Apps section of the Amazon Appstore for Android.

  • 2. Do I need a subscription to read NYTimes content on my Kindle?
    Back to top

    On the NYTimes app to your Kindle Fire, some content will be free. On the Kindle e-reader, you can enjoy a 14-day free trial. However, for unlimited access to all NYTimes content that is available, you must subscribe. For more information on digital subscriptions, see the Digital Subscriptions and Premium Products FAQ.

  • 3. Do I need to register with The New York Times to use the NYTimes Kindle application?
    Back to top

    No. You can download the NYTimes Kindle Fire application and access some content without registering. However, certain features — like saving and sharing articles — requires registration (registering is not the same as a subscription; you will not be charged for registering on NYTimes.com). For more information on creating an NYTimes.com account, see our Registration FAQ.

  • 4. I purchased an NYTimes subscription for my Kindle e-reader – do I get access to NYTimes.com and the NYTimes Kindle Fire app?
    Back to top

    Yes. After you link your Kindle e-reader subscription to your NYTimes.com account, you will have unlimited access to both NYTimes.com and the NYTimes app on your Kindle Fire.

  • 5. How do I use the NYTimes app for Kindle Fire?
    Back to top

    Besides some minor differences, the functionality is the same as the NYTimes app for Android — if you have questions about how to operate the app (for example, saving or sharing articles), please see the Android FAQ.

    The NYTimes app for Kindle Fire differs from the Android app in the following ways:

    • The Kindle Fire app does not allow widgets
    • There are no breaking news alerts on the Kindle Fire
  • 6. What if I have feedback or questions about the NYTimes on Kindle?
    Back to top

    We are very interested in hearing from our readers. Please submit feedback and/or questions to android@nytimes.com.
