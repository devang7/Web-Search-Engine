  • 1. Does it cost anything to use the NYTimes application for BlackBerry 10?
    Back to top

    The NYTimes application is free to download and install, and includes some content free. However, to get unlimited access to all sections you must become a digital subscriber. For more information, please see the Digital Subscriptions and Premium Products FAQ.

    Please note that in the absence of a Wi-Fi connection, the NYTimes app may use your device's cellular network connection to access and download content. Please check with your carrier to see if you will be charged for usage, especially while traveling.

  • 2. What devices will the NYTimes application for BlackBerry 10 work on?
    Back to top

    The NYTimes application works on BlackBerry devices running operating system version 10 or later. To determine your device's OS, open the Settings app and select About then scroll to the OS Version section.

  • 3. How do I get the NYTimes application?
    Back to top

    ​You can get the NYTimes application from the BlackBerry World app on your device.

  • 4. How do I navigate between articles and sections?
    Back to top

    From an article:
    To navigate back to a section front, tap anywhere on the article to bring up the bottom toolbar, then tap Back.

    You can also swipe from left edge of the screen to be taken back to the section front.

    From a section front:
    To see a list of all sections, tap the section front button on the left side of the bottom toolbar.

    You can also swipe from the left edge of the screen to see a list of all sections.

  • 5. How do I use the Saved list?
    Back to top

    You can access the Saved list from the main toolbar. From Saved, you can view the articles you have saved to your account from NYTimes.com or any other device.

    How to save an article »

  • 6. How do I save an article?
    Back to top

    You can save an article by adding it to the Saved list. You must be logged in to your NYTimes.com account to save an article. Saved articles will also be added to the Saved section in the NYTimes app of any other devices you own, as long as you are logged in on there as well. You can also access your saved articles at www.nytimes.com/saved

    To save an article, open the content you want to save in full article view. Next, tap the folder icon on the bottom toolbar. To remove a saved item, tap the folder icon on the toolbar while viewing that item.

  • 7. How do I change the font size?
    Back to top

    While reading an article, tap the button on the bottom toolbar, then tap the A icon to change the font size.

  • 8. How do I update the content on the app?
    Back to top

    To update the news content on your app, swipe down from the top edge of the screen and tap Refresh.

  • 9. How do I log in to my NYTimes.com account?
    Back to top

    Swipe down from the top edge of the screen and tap Settings, then tap the Log In or Create an Account button.

  • 10. Does news in the app update automatically? Can I change how often it updates?
    Back to top

    Yes — you can choose to have content updated automatically and set an interval for updates.

    Swipe down from the top edge of the screen and tap Settings. From the Settings page, open the Refresh drop-down menu and select how often you want news to automatically update.

  • 11. What if I have feedback or questions about the BlackBerry 10 NYTimes app?
    Back to top

    We are very interested in hearing from our readers. Please submit feedback and/or questions to bbapp@nytimes.com.
