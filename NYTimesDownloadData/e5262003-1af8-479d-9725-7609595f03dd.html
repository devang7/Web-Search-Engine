You will receive four coasters, each 4" square, which combine to make an 8" x 8" nautical chart. Approximately 100 charts of significant U.S. coastal waterway are currently available.

The nautical charts show depth information and the outlines of hidden sandbars.

The coasters are carved from large Botticino marble blocks and then tumbled, giving them a charming rustic look with worn surface and edges. No two stones are alike, making this a one-of-a-kind keepsake with far more character than found on plain ceramic tiles. The marbles natural veining, small fissures, coloring and tumbling all add to the beauty of this natural stone.

Handcrafted in New England from marble from the Mediterranean region. Comes in an attractive box to protect your marble coasters when not used. Each coaster has cork backing, to prevent scratching.

Please allow 5 business days for production.
