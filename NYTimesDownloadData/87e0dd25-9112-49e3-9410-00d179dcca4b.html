Deadly Earthquake in the Philippines

By REUTERS and THE ASSOCIATED PRESS | Feb. 11, 2017 | 0:28

At least six people were killed and more than 100 others were injured when a magnitude 6.7 earthquake struck the southern Philippines, northeast of the city of Surigao, late Friday, a disaster management official said on Saturday.
